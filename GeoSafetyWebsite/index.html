Our Principles

GeoSafety is an application and a startup with one clear principle: keep simple, be different. The concept of GeoSafety may not seem complex, however GeoSafety allows us to differentiate from crime statistics or common navigation applications and integrate these two aspects.

What we do

A geolocation-tracking app that sends a notification when a user enters a low safety zone and suggests an alternate route, accounting for safer routes and recommending popular tourist destinations.
